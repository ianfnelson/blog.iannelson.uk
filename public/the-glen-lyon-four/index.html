The Glen Lyon Four

May 30, 1999

A hot spring day taking in a circuitous route around the Glen Lyon four.

  • Carn Gorm (1029m)
  • Meall Garbh (968m)
  • Carn Mairg (1041m)
  • Meall nan Aighean (Creag Mhor) (981m)
