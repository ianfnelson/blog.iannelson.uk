A superlative day in the hills with Richard, Melanie, Hugh and Harriet.

A cloudy start at low heights, but as we ascended higher we broke through a cloud inversion, being presented with stunning blue skies and incredible views across to Ben Nevis and the Carn Mor Dearg arete.<figure class="kg-card kg-gallery-card kg-width-wide">
